Support the Archive

Free to use.
Not free to run.

Marq'd will always be free to read and contribute to — that's a commitment, not a marketing line. But the infrastructure that keeps the archive alive costs real money. Your support funds a project that doesn't answer to advertisers, investors, or anyone but the community it serves.

Why It Matters

An archive that lasts
requires real investment.

Queer history has been systematically erased — letters burned, diaries destroyed, institutions defunded, and websites scrubbed. What survives is a fraction of what existed. Marq'd exists to make sure that doesn't happen again.

Building something that lasts means paying for servers, storage, moderation, security, legal protections, and eventually, the people who maintain it. This doesn't happen on good intentions alone.

Every dollar donated goes directly toward the infrastructure and development that keeps this archive alive, independent, and accessible — permanently.

"In 64 countries, same-sex relationships are criminalized. In most of them, no official record of queer life exists. Marq'd is that record."

Marq'd operates transparently. Here is where the money goes, honestly and without spin. As the project grows, a full financial report will be published annually on Open Collective.

λ
Hosting & Infrastructure

Server costs, CDN, database hosting, and the compute that keeps the globe running.

λ
Storage

λ
Content Moderation

Keeping the archive safe and aligned with community values requires ongoing human review.

λ

Other Ways to Help

Marq'd is one person with a vision and a lot of work ahead. If you're a developer, designer, archivist, translator, human rights advocate, educator, or just someone who believes in this — reach out. We'd love to have you.
