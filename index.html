History

300 years of queer history,
on one globe.

Alongside user stories, Marq'd maps documented moments from queer history — legislative firsts, uprisings, tragedies, and cultural landmarks — sourced from academic research and verified historical records.

From France's 1791 decriminalization to India's 2018 Supreme Court ruling to events happening right now, the archive spans centuries and continents.

About

Built to last.
Built for everyone.

Marq'd was created because queer history disappears. Not just in countries where it's criminalized — everywhere. Stories get lost. Places get forgotten. People die and take their memories with them.

This archive is the antidote. Anonymous, permanent, and open to anyone who has a story and a place to attach it to.
